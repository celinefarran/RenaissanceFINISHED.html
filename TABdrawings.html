About

Drawing is the use of a utensil such as a pencil, pen, etc. to create a picture in varying shades of a single colour.

History

Earlier drawings were inscribed in walls and drawn on papers known as papyrus. Later drawing transferred to tablets made of natural materials such as wood and rock. Drawing has been a popular genre of art through centuries.
