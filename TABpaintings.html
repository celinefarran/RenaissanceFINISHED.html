About

Painting is an art created by a variety of pigments, otherwise known as paint, that is commonly used to create a picture.

History

Pigments have been made out of several things throughout history. Minerals and plants were commonly used as paint. Canvases ranged from a variety of surfaces such as rocks and cave walls.
